All about me

Hi. I am 17 this year, and I am studying for the Diploma of Infocomm Technology, or DIT, in Singapore Polytechnic at this moment. This very website you're seeing is a project as part of a course module I am taking, which is Front-End web Development, or FED.


Why Polytechnic?

I chose to go to polytechnic because I much prefer the hands-on project work compared to studying a textbook. There is absolutely nothing wrong about picking up a textbook and studying like that, but I have found that after 4 years in SST, I'd much prefer learning through observation then application.
